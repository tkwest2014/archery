Class List

  1. A101 Basic Archery:

    A basic archery course that focuses on beginning archery safety, skills, drills. Participants learn about archery form, range rules and etiquette. Form training and fun games are introduced in this class.

    Equipment: Provided with the exception of the safety kit.

    Cost: $120/month

    Schedule: Mondays and Wednesdays 4-5PM

    Prerequisite: Interest in learning about the sport of archery. Minimum age 8 years old or 7 years old with pre-evaluation.

  2. A102 Intermediate Archery:

    An intermediate archery course that focuses on intermediate archery safety, form, and introduces new equipment styles of shooting Recurve and Compound bows. Fun games and form reinforcement techniques are emphasized in this class.

    Equipment: Provided with the exception of the safety kit.

    Cost: $120/month

    Schedule: Tuesdays and Thursdays 4-5PM

    Prerequisite: A101 or recommendation from private lesson instructor. Minimum age 7 years old.

  3. A103 Advanced Archery:

    After taking A101 and A102 classes, students are eligible to participate in the A103 class. This class focuses on a continued education towards tournament styles of shooting used by many archery tournament associations from around the world. Along with the tournament lesson, participants receive continued knowledge around their shooting form and advice on personal equipment selections.

    Equipment: Provided with the exception of the safety kit.

    Cost: $120/month

    Schedule: Tuesdays and Thursdays 5-6PM

    Prerequisite: A101/A102 or equivalent. Minimum age 7 years old.

  4. Private Lessons:

    • Single Lesson

      1 hour of expert guidance by a USA Archery-certified coach.

      Cost: $50

    • 3 Lesson Package

      3 Lessons, 1 hour each of expert guidance by a USA Archery-certified coach.

      Cost: $140

    • 10 Lesson Package

      10 Lessons, 1 hour each of expert guidance by a USA Archery-certified coach.

      Cost: $380
